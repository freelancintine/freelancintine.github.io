  • EFFECTIVE USE OF MASKS:Though masks are a major equipment in prevention of Corona virus, if not used effectively can turn the tables other way round. It is necessary that mask being used fulfills the following parameters for use:
    • Fits snugly but comfortably against the side of the face and should cover nose and mouth perfectly with no exposure to the surroundings.
    • Be secured with ties or ear loops.

    • Includes multiple layers of fabric.
    • Allows for breathing without restriction.
    • Can be laundered and machine dried without damage or change to shape.
    • Is not touched with hands during it usage.
    • Make sure to not repeat the same mask after one usage. Wash the mask with soap by soaking in warm water and dry them in the sunlight.If possible use two masks alternatively day by day.
